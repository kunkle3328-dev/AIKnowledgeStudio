# AXIOM: Grounded Intelligence Studio

![AXIOM Cinematic Banner](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1639322537228-f710d846310a?auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=2000)

> **"Truth is not found in the void, but in the foundations of your data."**

AXIOM is a high-fidelity intelligence platform designed to transform raw, fragmented information into cohesive, grounded knowledge. Built on the cutting edge of the Gemini API, AXIOM serves as a "Grounded Intelligence Studio" where users can ingest multiple data sources and interact with them through cinematic audio overviews, multi-speaker podcasts, and real-time neural voice links.

## üõ∞Ô∏è Core Features

### 1. Intelligence Vaults (Projects)
Organize your research into secure, semantic "Vaults." Each vault uses a classification engine to determine its category‚ÄîTechnology, Science, Business, or Research‚Äîand adapts its cinematic UI skin and iconography to match the subject matter.

### 2. Multi-Source Neural Ingestion
AXIOM supports a wide array of high-bandwidth inputs:
*   **Physical Uplink**: Upload PDFs and local documents.
*   **Web Ingestion**: Direct syncing with URLs and research papers.
*   **Multimedia Sync**: Deep integration with YouTube transcripts and audio files.
*   **Manual Buffer**: Direct text pasting for rapid intelligence gathering.

### 3. Grounded Intelligence Chat
Interact with your data using a specialized chat interface. Every response is strictly grounded in your provided sources, using the **Gemini 3 Pro** model for advanced reasoning. The interface uses a specialized "Markdown-Lite" renderer to ensure high readability with cinematic typography.

### 4. Audio Studio: Narrative Synthesis
The crown jewel of AXIOM. Transform dry documents into a professional, two-voice podcast.
*   **Dual-Voice Logic**: Features a "Host" (Kore) and an "Analyst" (Puck) who discuss your sources with human-like nuance.
*   **Personality Profiles**: Choose between *Neutral, Curious, Analytical, Warm,* or *Debate* modes to change the tone of the conversation.
*   **Chapterized Progress**: Automatically generates thematic chapters with precise timestamps.

### 5. Direct Neural Link (Gemini Live)
Engage in a low-latency, real-time voice conversation with your data vault. Powered by the **Gemini 2.5 Flash Native Audio** model, this feature allows you to "talk" to your documents with human-level speed and expressive synthesis, perfect for hands-free study or rapid brainstorming.

### 6. Cinematic UI/UX
Designed with a "Tech-Noir" aesthetic:
*   **Axiom Design System**: Custom typography using *Syncopate* and *Space Grotesk*.
*   **Dynamic Waveforms**: Real-time visualizers that react to audio intensity and AI "thinking" states.
*   **Metallic Shimmer Effects**: High-end UI transitions and shimmering text treatments for a premium, professional feel.

*   **Audio**: Web Audio API with raw PCM 16-bit decoding and smoothing algorithms.
